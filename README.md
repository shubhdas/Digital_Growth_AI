# Digital Growth AI – CRM Automation Platform

AI-driven CRM platform designed to automate customer engagement,
lead management, and service operations.

## Business Problem
Companies struggle to manage leads, service cases, and customer communication
across multiple channels.

This project demonstrates how a CRM solution can automate:
- Lead tracking
- Customer support
- AI based insights

## Solution
This project implements a CRM system integrated with AI services
to automate lead qualification, support insights, and customer data analysis.

## Key Features
• Lead Management
• Customer Service Case Tracking
• AI Insights for Support
• Automated Notifications
• CRM Data Integration
